# AnyBook

A multi-business appointment booking app (salon, clinic, gym, and more) with separate client and business owner logins.

## Features

- Separate login flows for Clients and Business Owners
- Browse businesses and book appointments
- View booking confirmations and manage upcoming/past bookings
- Business owner dashboard to view and manage appointments
- Add, edit, and manage services offered
- Business setup and profile settings

## Tech Stack

- Kotlin
- Jetpack Compose
- Spring Boot
